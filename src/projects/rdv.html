Type: Company project (Squiz)

Scope: Interactive map script, postcode search, list of regional cities, CMS Implementation
Technologies: HTML, JS, Matrix CMS, Google Maps API, .geojson

Objective

All of the following three sections are connected to each other and serve one purpose - provide informations about offices in your region.

Interactive map of Australia

The first section is an interactive map with regions of Australia. Clicking one zooms in and show it's subregions.

Clicking subregion shows relative informations about it in the section below.

Postcode search

It's a quick way to find information you need: just four digits

List of cities

The last part is a big list of cities, with dynamic filter on the left.
